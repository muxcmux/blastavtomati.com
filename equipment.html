Equipment and machinery

At Devshard we have years of experience in designing, building and maintaining plastics processing equipment. Injection moulding, material recycling, automatic waste removal and various other production automation machinery - we have done it all

KAMP-01 BLOW MOULDING MACHINE

Devshard's flagship product. The design was concieved in the 90's and has undergone several iterations since then to bring it up to speed with latest tech.

KAMP-01 Blow-moulding machine is a piece of equipment for the production of hollow volume articles from PVC, PE and PP with capacity from 5ml to 150ml. Its productivity is 300 items per hour when manufacturing 100ml bottles and it can be plugged in a standard 220 V wall socket, which makes it an exceptional performer and first in it's class.

The machine's screw pack is driven by an asynchronous motor with a reduction gear transmission. The motor revolutions are being gauged, controlled and adjusted by a programmable control block. The drive is built on LENZ - Austria components. The control block is based on a programmable controller. The machine is operated from a control panel with sensor information available on an LCD display.

There are options for needle-forming plus automatic cutting off of the technological waste and mandrill. The command panel, pneumatic controllers and information components are made by FESTO - Austria.

KAMP-01 machine operates on a non-stop production cycle. The use of contemporary high tech components from renowned European companies secures reliability, flexibility and simplicity of the adjustments, control and operation of the machine. The machine achieves a lower then average cost of the manufactured items due to its low power consumption and high for its class productivity.

  • Screw length / diameter 22 D / 20 mm
  • Screw revolutions (adjustable) 0-100 rev/min
  • Power of cylinder heating 2.5 kW
  • Screw drive (asynchronous) 0.75 kW
  • Power voltage 220 V
  • Mould compartment drive pneumatic
  • Working air pressure 6-7 bar
  • Max closing force 300 N
  • Dimensions of the closing plate 260/120/20 mm
  • Dimensions 1500/860/1500 mm
